# Kanji Review Quiz — For Asahi 🌅

Hi Asahi!

One test score is not who you are. **56 points just means you found 22 kanji that are ready to become your new friends.** Every kanji you missed is one you can win back — and this little app will help you do it.

## What is this?

A quiz app made just for you, covering all the kanji from your first-term Grade 3 tests. Open `漢字みなおしテスト.html` in a browser and press スタート!

## How it works

- **20 questions** each time — 13 kanji questions and 7 okurigana (送りがな) questions
- Pick the right answer from **3 choices**
- You get ⭕ or ❌ right away, so you learn on the spot
- **5 points per question, 100 points total** — just like your school test
- At the end you see the kanji you missed, so you know exactly what to practice
- **Every round is different!** Questions are picked randomly from about 110 in total

## The Ranking

Your **best 5 scores** are saved on the results screen. You are not competing with anyone else — you are competing with *yesterday's Asahi*. Watch your name climb your own leaderboard. 🥇

## A small plan that works

1. Play **one round a day** — it only takes a few minutes
2. Look at the missed kanji at the end and write each one **once** on paper
3. Play again tomorrow — the app will quiz you on different questions

Small steps, every day. That is how 56 becomes 70, then 85, then 100.

You've got this. 💪
